Este código calcula la altura promedio de una planta en cada sitio:
 

```r
shrub_dims <- read.csv('shrub-volume-data.csv')
by_site <- group_by(shrub_dims, site)
avg_height <- summarize(by_site, avg_height = mean(height))
```
